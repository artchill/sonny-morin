La Historia · About the Book

A lifetime of journeys, told one chapter at a time

Spanning more than thirty years of unforgettable journeys, Adventures of a Chicano Spanish Teacher, Mexico and Beyond follows Sonny Morin's backpacking, scuba diving, and sightseeing adventures across the Western Hemisphere and Micronesia. From ancient ruins rising out of the jungle to vibrant city plazas alive with music and mercado chatter, every chapter captures the thrill of arriving somewhere new — and the deeper joy of understanding it.

Beneath the surface, the adventure continues. Morin descends into crystal-clear waters teeming with life, trading the classroom for coral reefs and cenotes, and returning with stories most travelers only dream about.

Blending personal reflection with pulse-quickening experience, this memoir is more than a travelogue. It is an open invitation — from a teacher who spent his career opening doors to language and culture — to share in a lifetime of adventure, one border crossing, one dive, one unforgettable meal at a time.

El Autor · About the Author

SONNY MORIN

Retired Spanish teacher · Travel memoirist · Central Texas

Cartografía de una vida · The map of a life

Sonny Morin is a retired Spanish teacher and travel memoirist from Central Texas. Raised in a small South Texas town, he earned a Master of Arts in Teaching from Texas State University and dedicated his career to teaching Spanish — and to sharing his lifelong passion for language, history, and culture.

As a Chicano educator, he spent more than three decades exploring Mexico and destinations far beyond it, carrying his classroom's curiosity everywhere his backpack and dive gear could take him.

Since retiring in 2017, he has kept writing. He is currently at work on a second book — a reflection on his years in the classroom and the students who made them unforgettable.
